Babysitter Kata

Background
----------
This kata simulates a babysitter working and getting paid for one night.  The rules are pretty straight forward:

The babysitter 
- starts no earlier than 5:00PM
- leaves no later than 4:00AM
- gets paid $12/hour from start-time to bedtime
- gets paid $8/hour from bedtime to midnight
- gets paid $16/hour from midnight to end of job
- gets paid for full hours (no fractional hours)

Feature:
As a babysitter
In order to get paid for 1 night of work
I want to calculate my nightly charge

Notes:
To run this app you can go into these folders and locate the .exe file

PillarTechnology-Kata / PillarTechnology / Kata_BabySitterPayCalculator / Kata_BabySitterPayCalculator / bin / Debug / PayCalculator.exe

or you can simply load the solution in Visual studios and run it.

When it runs you will be required to enter in the details asked:
- Total hours worked
- if the child had a bedtime and if so hours worked before the child's bedtime
- if you worked after midnght, and if so how many hours did you work after midnight.
